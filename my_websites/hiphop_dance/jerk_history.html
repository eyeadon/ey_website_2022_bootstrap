Jerk History

Jerk dance evolved from a 2009 song by the New Boyz entitled "You're a Jerk". The dance starts by moving your legs in and out at the knees. Dancers step front to back, alternating directions in which they step. Jerk dance was influenced by the Boogaloo style. Hip Hop is the Jerker's music of choice. Boogaloo dancers hit certain rhythms and steps that blended well with Hip Hop music. Jerkers created new steps that blended the old style with the new.

What's Happening

Jerk has been featured in music videos, TV shows, and publicized dance competitions. Hip Hop dance is growing in popularity. Many young people are now dancing because of videos seen on YouTube.

Ultimately Hip Hop dance is a way to express yourself and enjoy music. It is great exercise and relieves stress. Dance is a great way to connect with others and share the same groove. As a dancer, you are allowed to experiment and develop your own style. Your style is an extension of you.
